## Navigation

### Home Page
- Post feed with all posts
- **Search** button - Find users
- **Profile** button - View your profile  
- **Logout** button - Sign out

### Search Page
- Search box to find users by username
- Grid of user cards with stats
- Quick access back to **Home**

### Profile Page
- Your own or other user's profile
- See all their posts
- Follow/Unfollow button (if viewing others)
- **Back to Home** and **Search** buttons

## Example Workflow

1. **Sign up/Login** on the auth page
2. **Create posts** on the home page
3. **Click Search** to find other users
4. **View their profiles** by clicking the "View Profile" button
5. **Follow** users you like
6. **Visit your profile** to see your stats
7. See your **followers** and **following** count grow!

## User Search Features

- **Case-insensitive** - Search "john" or "JOHN", both work
- **Partial matches** - Search "jo" to find "john"
- **Limits results** - Shows up to 20 matching users
- **Real-time** - Results update as you search

## Profile Information

Each user profile shows:
- **Avatar** - First letter of username in a gold circle
- **Username** - Their display name
- **Bio** - Optional bio text
- **Post Count** - Number of posts they've made
- **Followers** - People following them
- **Following** - People they follow

## Follow System

When you follow someone:
- Their follower count increases
- You appear in their followers list
- Your following count increases
- They appear in your following list
- You can unfollow anytime
